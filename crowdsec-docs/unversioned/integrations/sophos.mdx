The CrowdSec Sophos integration connects CrowdSec's hosted blocklist endpoint to your Sophos firewall.  
Sophos calls this feature **Third-Party Threat Feeds**, which enable you to integrate external sources of IP addresses, domains, and URLs involved in threat activity.

:::info
Ensure your Sophos device supports Third-Party Threat Feeds.  
The vendor documentation is available in the [References](#references) section below.
:::

## Step 1 — Create the integration in the CrowdSec Console

In the [Integrations](https://app.crowdsec.net/blocklists/integrations) page, click **Connect** under the Sophos card.

<ThemedImage
    alt="Sophos Integration Card"
    sources={{

        dark: useBaseUrl("/img/console_integrations_sophos_card_dark.png"),

Name the integration (must be unique to your account), then click **Create**.

<ThemedImage
    alt="Sophos Integration Creation Screen"

        dark: useBaseUrl("/img/console_integrations_creation_dark.png"),

:::warning
The credentials shown next are displayed **only once**. Copy them before closing this screen.
:::

<ThemedImage
    alt="Sophos Integration Credentials Screen"

You now have an HTTPS endpoint and Basic Auth credentials to configure on your Sophos device.
